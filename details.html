About The Sonnet

Located in the heart of Jamshedpur, The Sonnet offers a perfect blend of luxury and comfort. Our hotel features elegantly designed rooms, a state-of-the-art fitness center, an outdoor swimming pool, and multiple dining options.

Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, our dedicated staff is committed to providing you with an unforgettable experience. Enjoy our complimentary high-speed WiFi, 24-hour room service, and convenient access to the city's major attractions.
